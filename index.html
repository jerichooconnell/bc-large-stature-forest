Definitions. "Large-stature old growth" = VRI polygons with projected age >150 yr and SITE_INDEX at or above a flat cutoff of – (mean height at 50 yr breast-height age), the same cutoff applied to all three zones. The historical extent is this same old-growth total plus stands on equivalently high-site ground that carry an explicit harvest record (HARVEST_DATE / opening indicators) — it excludes naturally young, never-logged stands so natural disturbance history isn't counted as loss.
